The Person That Keeps Babylon's Fall alive

One is the loneliest number... , Image by: PlatinumGames

Square Enix's live service loot slasher game hits a new all time low on PC.

Babylon's Fall, the troubled live-service action-RPG from action game luminaries Platinum Games, has seemingly hit life support. After two months on the market and a lukewarm reception at launch, Square Enix's latest effort was reduced to just one player on PC last week.
